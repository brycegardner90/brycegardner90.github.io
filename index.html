About Me

Analyst with a business-first mindset

I hold a BBA from the University of Georgia (3.91 GPA, Summa Cum Laude) and a Google Data Analytics Certificate, and I've spent the past few years building a full analytics portfolio from scratch — no tutorials, just real datasets and real questions.

My background in operations management gave me a feel for what business stakeholders actually need from data: not just what happened, but why it happened and what to do about it. I write up every project with that lens — findings and recommendations, not just charts.

I work across the full pipeline: Python for cleaning and analysis, SQLite for structured querying, and Tableau or Power BI for visualization. Every project is documented on GitHub with clean code and a published dashboard.
